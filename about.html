I'm Blake Rochelle, an interaction designer

I grew up in Alabama and graduated from Auburn University with a bachelors in Information Technology. I began my career focusing on web design and front end developemnt before my role gradually dovetailed with my interests in UX design/research, story telling and creating delightful experiences for the user. I have learned how to observe, discover, predict and design for human behavior and the experiences in our everyday living & digital environments. I believe in creating engaging user experiences in the digital world while pushing boundaries; I love to create clean, subdued yet immersive experiences with everyday products and services. I love seeing how advancements in technology affect how we adapt and interact with machines and with each other. My work heavily focuses on the user's experience with the product.

As a regular person, I enjoy: enjoying music, the travel, eating every food that I possibly can, hanging out with my kid, Cafe Bustelo by the pot, making dumb lists & other factoids that just wasted valuable seconds of your life ;)
