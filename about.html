I'm Blake Rochelle, an interaction designer

I grew up in Alabama and graduated from Auburn University with a bachelors in Information Technology. I began my career focusing on web design and front end development before my role gradually dovetailed with my interests in User Experience design & research, story telling and creating delightful experiences for the user. My experience and training have taught me how to observe, discover, predict and design for human behavior and the experiences in our everyday living & digital environments. I believe in creating engaging user experiences in the digital world while pushing boundaries; my passion is creating clean, subdued yet immersive experiences with everyday products and services. I also love seeing how advancements in technology affect how we adapt and interact with the digital world and with each other.

In my spare time, I enjoy music (listening, discovering, creating and experiencing), travel & exploring, eating any and every food that I can get my hands on, hanging out with my beautiful wife & daughter, and Cafe Bustelo by the pot
